AIR CANADA BEREAVEMENT TRAVEL POLICY
Effective: Current

Air Canada offers reduced bereavement fares to assist customers travelling due to the death or imminent death of an immediate family member. This policy is designed to provide flexibility and support during difficult times.

Eligibility

Bereavement fares are available to passengers travelling because of the death, or imminent death, of an immediate family member. Immediate family is defined as: spouse or common-law partner, child or stepchild, parent or stepparent, sibling or stepsibling, grandparent, grandchild, parent-in-law, sibling-in-law, son- or daughter-in-law, aunt, uncle, niece, nephew, or first cousin.

How to Book

Bereavement fares must be booked by contacting Air Canada Reservations directly by telephone at 1-888-247-2262. Bereavement fares cannot be booked online, through travel agents, or through the chatbot service. When booking, the passenger must provide the name of the deceased or terminally ill family member, the relationship to the passenger, and the name and contact details of the funeral home, hospital, or attending physician. Air Canada Reservations may verify this information.

Important: Refunds and Retroactive Application

Bereavement fares must be requested AT THE TIME OF BOOKING. Air Canada does not offer retroactive bereavement discounts. Tickets purchased at full fare cannot be reclassified as bereavement fares after the journey has been completed, regardless of the circumstances of travel. This policy applies without exception.

Documentation

Passengers may be asked to provide documentation supporting their bereavement claim, such as a death certificate, a letter from a funeral home, or a letter from an attending physician confirming imminent death. Documentation may be requested up to 90 days after travel.

Routes and Restrictions

Bereavement fares apply to travel within Canada and between Canada and the United States. Bereavement fares are not available for international routes outside of North America. Standard baggage allowances and fare conditions apply. Bereavement fares are subject to seat availability at the time of booking.
