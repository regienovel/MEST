AIR CANADA REFUND FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I get a refund for a flight I no longer wish to take?

Refundability depends on the fare class you purchased. Flex and higher fare classes are generally refundable, while Standard and Basic fares are typically non-refundable. Please check the fare conditions associated with your booking. To request a refund, contact Air Canada Reservations at 1-888-247-2262 or submit a refund request through the Air Canada website's refund request form.

What is the deadline to request a refund?

Refund requests must generally be submitted within 24 months of the original ticket issue date. However, certain refund types have shorter deadlines. Bereavement fare refund requests are not accepted under any circumstances, as bereavement fares must be booked at the time of original purchase.

Can I get a partial refund if my fare class allowed me to fly at a discount I did not initially claim?

No. Air Canada does not offer retroactive fare adjustments. The fare paid at the time of booking is the final fare for that ticket. Discounts that were available but not requested at the time of booking cannot be applied after travel has been completed.

What if a customer service representative or chatbot told me I could claim a discount retroactively?

Air Canada's published policies are the authoritative source for all fare and refund rules. If you have received conflicting information from any source, please refer to the official policy pages on the Air Canada website or contact Air Canada Reservations directly to confirm the correct information before making travel decisions.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refund requests are typically processed within 4 to 6 weeks of receipt. Refunds are issued to the original form of payment used to purchase the ticket. You will receive an email confirmation when the refund has been processed.

What documentation do I need to submit a refund request?

You will need your booking reference (six characters), the name of the passenger as it appears on the ticket, and the original form of payment. For certain refund types, additional documentation may be required, such as medical certificates for medical refund requests or proof of military orders for military refund requests.
