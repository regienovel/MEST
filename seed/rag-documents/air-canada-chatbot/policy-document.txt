AIR CANADA — BEREAVEMENT TRAVEL POLICY
Effective Date: January 1, 2022
Last Updated: October 15, 2022

1. ELIGIBILITY

Air Canada offers reduced bereavement fares for passengers travelling due to the imminent death or recent passing of an immediate family member. Immediate family includes spouse, parent, child, sibling, grandparent, grandchild, parent-in-law, or legal guardian.

2. HOW TO REQUEST A BEREAVEMENT FARE

Passengers must contact Air Canada Reservations directly by telephone BEFORE booking their flight. Bereavement fares cannot be booked online, through the mobile app, or through third-party travel agencies.

The reservations agent will:
- Verify the relationship to the deceased or critically ill family member
- Confirm available flights and applicable bereavement fare pricing
- Book the ticket at the reduced fare

3. REQUIRED DOCUMENTATION

Within 14 days of travel, the passenger must provide:
- A copy of the death certificate, or a signed letter from a physician or hospital for cases of imminent death
- Proof of relationship to the deceased or critically ill family member

Failure to provide documentation may result in the fare being adjusted to the full published fare.

4. FARE STRUCTURE

Bereavement fares typically offer a discount of approximately 10-15% off the lowest available full-fare economy or business class ticket at the time of booking. Exact discounts vary by route and availability.

5. IMPORTANT LIMITATIONS

- Bereavement fares MUST be requested BEFORE the flight is booked.
- Retroactive requests for bereavement discounts on already-purchased tickets are NOT accepted under any circumstances.
- Bereavement fares cannot be combined with other promotional offers, loyalty program discounts, or Aeroplan redemptions.
- Changes and cancellations to bereavement fare tickets are subject to standard fare rules for the class of service booked.

6. CONTACT

To request a bereavement fare, call Air Canada Reservations:
- Within Canada and the US: 1-888-247-2262
- International callers: Visit aircanada.com for local numbers
- Available 24 hours, 7 days a week
